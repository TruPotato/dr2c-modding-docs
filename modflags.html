Modflag Dictionary


DR2C MOD_FLAG dictionary

USE_FILTER is a filter of the uses (like picking up objects, opening doors)

MOD_FLAGS correspond to STATMODS in the games files.
They are numbered in powers of 2.
For example, an id of 3 correlates to 2^3, or 8.
You can combine the numbers, to achieve several statmods.
For example, setting the MOD_FLAG of a character to be 257 (256 and 1) will give you both sick and alvis singing.
The nth power of 2 that is used for MOD_FLAGS correspods (I believe) to the position of their respective ENUM_STATMOD tag in charstatmods.df.
Below, you will find a moderately comprehensive list of MOD_FLAGS that are available to use.
MOD_FLAGS2 is an unused variable. Feel free to use it whatever way you desire.
It is not currently known what USE_INFO does but it is instrumental for some abilities.
Those marked with "No visible change" require further testing.
